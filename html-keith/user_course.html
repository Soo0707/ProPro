Proposal Guidelines

Welcome to Multimedia Fundamentals for the trimester 2430. As IT students, you may not be training in design or artistic skill, but so much of art, design and entertainment takes place through computers that a strong understanding of how these things are handled digitally is important in your studies. I also suspect that many of you are have at least some art or design talents and motivations, and like all things, these things can be learned through practice. So this course is an opportunity to put those leanings to practice. Looking forward to seeing all the amazing things you will create this trimester!
